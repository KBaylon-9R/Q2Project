What is Filipino Food?

Filipino cuisine is a vibrant and diverse culinary tradition that refelcts the country's rich cultural history and regional variations. it ofers a wide range of flavors and cooking techniques, from simple meals to elaborate dishes, and continues to evolve and adapt to new influences and context. The cuisine is unique blend of indigenous, Spanish, Chinese and American inlfuences.
